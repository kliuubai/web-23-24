The theremin is an instrument you play without touching.

  • It is an entirely electronic instrument.
  • It was invented by Leon Theremin in 1920.
  • It works similarly to a radio reciever.

Here's some basics on how to play the theremin.

  1. Put your dominant hand next to the pitch antenna.
  2. Put your nondominant hand next to the volume antenna.
  3. Reach away from the pitch antenna to produce lower notes; reach closer to produce higher notes.
  4. Reach away from the volume antenna to produce quieter sounds; reach towards to produce louder sounds.
